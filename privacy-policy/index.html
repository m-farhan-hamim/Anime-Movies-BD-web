Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 2026

1. Introduction

Thank you for using Anime Movies BD. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring a seamless, safe experience while you search for and watch your favorite anime. This policy explains exactly how we handle the minimal data we receive when you interact with our Telegram Mini App.

2. Data We Collect

We only access basic info provided by the Telegram API:

  • ▹ Telegram ID: To verify channel subscriptions.
  • ▹ Profile Info: (Name/Username) Used only if you submit a manual report to admins.
  • ▹ Searches: Anonymous movie requests to help us add new content.

We do not store your data in any external database.

3. Third-Party Services

To keep the bot free, we rely on external services:

  • Monetag: Used to display ads. Monetag may use cookies or device data to serve relevant ads.
  • Terabox: Hosts our anime. Clicking watch links redirects you to Terabox.
  • Telegram: Our infrastructure runs within Telegram's secure ecosystem.

4. Security & Contact

Your privacy is our priority. Since we do not maintain a permanent user database, your profile information is inherently protected by Telegram's native end-to-end encryption and security protocols. We will never sell or share your data with marketing agencies.

Need help or have questions? Reach out to our admins directly via the Report button inside the Anime Movies BD Mini App.
