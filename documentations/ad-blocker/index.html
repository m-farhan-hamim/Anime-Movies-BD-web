Ad Loading Solutions

Follow these steps if ads are not loading in our Telegram bot.

Basic Troubleshooting

  • 1

    Turn off Ad Blockers

    If you are using any ad-blocker extensions (like uBlock Origin) or ad-blocking browsers (like Brave), please disable them or whitelist our site.

  • 2

    Disable Ad-Blocking DNS

    Go to your phone's settings > Network & Internet > Private DNS. If you are using an ad-blocking DNS (like dns.adguard.com), change it to 'Automatic' or turn it off.

  • 3

    Clear Cache & Retry

    Clear your browser or Telegram app cache, then restart the bot and try generating the link again.
